How to add to iPhone
Step by step · iOS Calendar
1
Tap Download Calendar File above. Safari will download the .ics file.
2
A prompt appears: "cedar-foraging-calendar.ics — Do you want to add this calendar?" — tap Allow.
3
iOS Calendar opens and shows a preview. Tap Add All to import all 370 events. They're added to a new calendar called Cedar · NorCal Foraging Seasons.
4
Open Calendar app → tap the calendar list → find Cedar · NorCal Foraging Seasons. The color is set to Cedar gold automatically.
5
Done. Every species season appears on your calendar and repeats automatically every year. Tap any event to see the full field notes.
